MLC IMPORTANT INFORMATION

  • The wristband and main hub of the festival will be located at The Station and will be open from 11.30am till 8.30pm.
  • Doors to SWX will open at 2pm.
  • There are 2 NCP car parks near the festival, one located on All Saints Street and one located on Rupert Street.
  • Please be careful when walking along the main roads, there will be cars and vans moving around all day.
  • Each venue reserves the right to refuse service of alcohol behind the bars. Please bring a valid form of I.D (Passport/Driving License/National ID card/etc) if intending to purchase alcohol at one of the venues.
  • There is a cloakroom available at SWX only.
  • Afterparty is strictly 18+ and located in The Lanes from 10pm-3am.
