The Station

Venue Website

The Station has lift access to the first, second and third floors. There are accessible toilets located on each floor. The front of the venue has paved access and on-street parking directly outside of the main entrance (unrestricted with a blue badge).

When attending a large event at the Station there is step free access available on Rupert Street into our main space. If you would like to use this entrance, please either call ahead or come to our main reception desk (via the step free entrance on Silver street) and a member of staff can assist you. The Performance Space floor has good sight lines and good visibility on the stage depending on crowd density and set up. The stage can be set at different heights (maximum 1 metre). If a smoking area is being used, this is wheelchair accessible. If you would like to discuss the best options for your visit to the venue, please get in touch or speak to a member of venue staff on arrival.

Seating arrangements for theatre, music and dance performances vary and we unfortunately do not have a viewing platform.

Please contact us if you have any questions about the set-up for a particular event.

SWX

Venue Website

SWX is a multistorey venue. We have a passenger lift which is located at the back of the venue within a staff only area. If you need to use the lift please contact us in advance to arrange access.

Carer ticket holders should be able to meet the needs and assist the person they are accompanying in the event of an emergency.
